Betrayal




From the dark black ball, I see,

Knives were like pointing at me.

Sweet word from his mouth flattered me,

Yet, doubtful as it can be.

Angel, he looks when in front of you,

Demon when behind laughing at you.

A friend you've thought to trust,

But an enemy you never want.

Like a snake from the wild,

Keeps gossiping behind.

Like predator that hunts its prey,

It never stops until it ends its day.

His words may be dear,

But actions are more clear.

Be careful with your friends,

You might be stabbed at the back at the end.
