I like finding mysteries like a pro. Few more things about me is that I am into fangirling. Just like all the other teens out there, I am taking a part of my time adoring bands and groups. Listening to musics and watching random videos are included in the list, as well, as they are my way of relaxation. All these kind of things are just my way to take my time off. Hence, they are just for a leisure since being a student takes the biggest part of my time. However, nothing ever competes a person with interest especially when it gives him/her a full blast of life.
